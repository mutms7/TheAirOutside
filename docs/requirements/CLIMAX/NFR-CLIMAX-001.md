No minimum dwell time is enforced at the climax pause; the story advances to the next line immediately on any click regardless of how long the player waited.

Verification: Manual — reach the climax pause, click immediately (under 1s), and confirm the story proceeds to the next scene identically to waiting 30s before clicking.
